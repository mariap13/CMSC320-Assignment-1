Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the entire history of your repository (safe option), 
while rebasing moves your branch onto the tip of main creating a form of
linear history. Therefore, merging is combining two histories while rebasing
is rewriting hsitory.
